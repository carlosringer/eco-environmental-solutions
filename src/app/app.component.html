About Us

Our Mission

Established in 2013, Eco-Environmental Solutions eradicates abandoned, blighted, and fire damaged properties in urban communities throughout metro Detroit. We accomplish this through deconstruction a “green building” technology. The 90% hands-on process reduces construction and demolition waste contributing to landfill usage, reuses and recycles building materials, and employs individuals centrally located in these same affected communities. We feel this empowers current and future residents with an up-close and personal view of the economic and social wealth building opportunities created in the midst of the blight!

Why Choose Us?

We are a grassroots organization that is focused on addressing blight and stewarding green building practices throught metro Detroit. Helping city residents get a chance to see people from similar backgrounds as themselves cleaning up neighborhood blight has been a powerful incentive and drives our efforts everyday.

Eco-Environmental Solutions aims to reuse or repurpose around 60% of anything it touches during a deconstruction endeavor.
